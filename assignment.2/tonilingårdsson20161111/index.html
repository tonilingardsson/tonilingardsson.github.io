Evolution

A brieve description of how video games were, are & will be.

Past

Despite the first videogames were invented in the 50's, the 70's is the decade of its commercial boom. Atari, Sega, Taito are names of companies that became popular in the 70's with their games. They are no longer leading the industry, nevertheless their games a tribute of cult.

Present

Nowadays the videogame industry takes the entertainment beyond the game. The Internet promoted the dissemination of online games, gamers communities and interactivity with the user. More than just sit & play.

Future

Virtual reality (VR) is a realistic simulation of user's presence in a 3D environment, created using interactive software and hardware, controlled by movement of the body and enabling the user to interact with space, objects, sounds and other sensations replicated therein. Today is believed it will be the future.
